# Campaign Finance Report — Q3 2024

## Overview

The quarterly campaign finance report for Council Member Jane Rodriguez shows a significant donation received on July 15, 2024, from Sunrise Development Group LLC in the amount of $25,000. This represents the largest single donation received by the council member during the current election cycle.

## Donor Background

Sunrise Development Group LLC is a real estate development firm registered in the state in March 2019. The principal owner is Marcus Chen, who has contributed to multiple city council campaigns over the past five years. Chen's firm specializes in mixed-use residential and commercial developments in the downtown corridor.

## Timeline of Events

- **March 2019**: Sunrise Development Group LLC registered with the Secretary of State.
- **July 15, 2024**: $25,000 donation made to Council Member Rodriguez's re-election campaign.
- **August 3, 2024**: Rodriguez voted against a zoning variance application submitted by Westside Properties, a competing developer.
- **September 12, 2024**: Sunrise Development Group was awarded a $2.3 million contract for the Riverfront Redevelopment Project.
- **September 20, 2024**: Rodriguez participated in a ceremonial groundbreaking for the Riverfront project.

## Voting Record

Council Member Rodriguez has served on the Zoning and Development Committee since January 2022. During her tenure, she has voted on 14 zoning-related contracts. Records show she voted against competing firms' applications on 3 occasions while voting in favor of applications from firms that had previously donated to her campaign on 8 occasions.

## Recusal Records

According to city clerk records, Council Member Rodriguez did not file a recusal notice for the September 12, 2024, contract vote. The city's ethics guidelines recommend recusal when a council member has received campaign contributions exceeding $5,000 from a contractor within the preceding 12-month period.

## Financial Details

The Riverfront Redevelopment Project contract includes:
- Base contract value: $2,300,000
- Performance bonus provisions: up to $400,000
- Estimated total value over 3 years: $3,500,000
